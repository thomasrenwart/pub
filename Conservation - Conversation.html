Conservation

By Thomas Renwart, 3-12-2016

Conservation consists of a series of works where I go and try to capture and conservate botanicals paired with textiles to commemorate a collection of gathered objects. The starting point was the word Timescape, whereas three words can be directed from; time, escape and landscape. Starting with dissecting these words through written dialogues leading to further growth resulting is small, pocketsize sculptures that makes one recall a childhood memory where we used to conserve butterflies or bees in matchboxes.
